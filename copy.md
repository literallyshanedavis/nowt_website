## Why iPad
*The differentiation pitch. Every competitor is desktop/web. This section explains why iPad matters.*

- **Eyebrow:** Why iPad
- **Headline:** Built for touch first.

### Feature 1: Touch-native node editing
Drag nodes with your fingers. Connect them with a swipe. Pinch to zoom the canvas. No mouse required, no browser fighting you.

### Feature 2: Apple Pencil in the pipeline
Sketch directly into ControlNet. Draw masks for inpainting. Annotate outputs by hand. Your pencil is a node input.

### Feature 3: Camera as a node
Point your iPad at something, capture it, and it's in your pipeline. Product photos, reference shots, textures — zero-friction input from the real world.

### Feature 4: On-device when it counts
Small models run locally via CoreML. Work on a train. Work in a field. The heavy models hit the cloud; everything else stays on your iPad.

## What is nowt?
*A brief, confident explanation of what the product is and how it differs.*

**Body text:**
Every creative AI tool is a chat box or a settings panel. You type a prompt, cross your fingers, and get one thing back. Nowt is different.
