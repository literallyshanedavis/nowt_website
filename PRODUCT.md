# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Creative AI users who want visual workflows without being tied to a desktop: designers, image makers, small studios, filmmakers, prompt-heavy experimenters, and iPad-native creative workers. They arrive curious, visually led, and impatient with generic AI landing pages.

## Product Purpose

thirtyseven is an iPad-first visual AI creative workflow app. The landing page should make the product feel cinematic, tactile, capable, and specific: compose AI workflows on an infinite canvas, run them with a tap, and reuse the creative process later.

## Brand Personality

Warm, dry, understated, confident. Northern English in tone without performing it. The page should feel cinematic and calm, not corporate or shouty.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI SaaS pages, gradient text, icon-card grids, center-stacked hero templates, desktop-only workflow metaphors, over-explained features, and any direct use of Wonder Studios assets or branding.

## Design Principles

Show the canvas as a physical creative space.

Make iPad mobility the emotional reason to care.

Use fewer words, but make each one concrete.

Borrow cinematic pacing and black-stage layout without copying third-party media.

Keep the early access action simple and close at hand.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, preserve readable type on dark backgrounds, support reduced motion preferences, keep focus states visible, and avoid requiring hover-only interactions.
